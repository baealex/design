# AI Output Checklist

Before finalizing a UI/FE result, verify:

- [ ] `design profile` is declared.
- [ ] `design refs` has 1-2 slugs only.
- [ ] Typography choices are derived from references.
- [ ] Color and surface choices are derived from references.
- [ ] Layout and spacing choices are derived from references.
- [ ] Motion and interaction choices are derived from references.
- [ ] Accessibility baseline is preserved (focus, keyboard, semantics).
- [ ] Final output follows `CLAUDE.md` and `.conor/CONOR.md`.
