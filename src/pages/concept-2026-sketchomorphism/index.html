Human-like Interface

Sketchomorphism

AI generates perfection.
We chose imperfection. On purpose.

Used in Ocean Brain

AI now generates flawless images, pixel-perfect layouts, and interfaces smoother than anything a human hand could draw. Everything has become polished, predictable, sterile. So we went the other way — intentionally imperfect interfaces. Lines that wobble like they were drawn by a human hand. Borders that redraw themselves, never the same twice. This isn't retro cosplay. It's a way of saying "a human made this" in the age of algorithms.
